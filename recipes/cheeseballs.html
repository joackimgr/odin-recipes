Cheese Balls

Description

These easy sausage balls with Cheddar are easy to make with baking mix. They are great for appetizers or for breakfast! They can be frozen before or after baking.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups sherred Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 3 cups baking mix

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil
  2. Mix cheese, sausage, and dry baking mix together in a large bowl until well combined; shape mixture into walnut-size balls. Place on prepared cookie sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 12 to 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometed inserted into the center should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C). Serve hot.
